Learn Stats! Resources

Here are some helpful websites, videos, workshops, and books to boost your statistics skills.

Websites

  • Statistics by Jim – Clear, accessible stats tutorials.
  • Statistics How To – Step-by-step guides for many concepts.

Videos & YouTube Channels

  • StatQuest with Josh Starmer – Excellent stats explanations.
  • CrashCourse Statistics – Entertaining and concise lessons.

Workshops & Courses

  • Coursera Statistics Specialization – Comprehensive online courses.
  • DataCamp Statistical Thinking – Interactive coding-focused stats.

Books

  • “Statistics Done Wrong” by Alex Reinhart – Common pitfalls and how to avoid them.
  • “Discovering Statistics Using IBM SPSS Statistics” by Andy Field – A classic for social sciences.
