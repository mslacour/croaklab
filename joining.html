Joining the CROAK Lab

Interested in joining our research team? We welcome motivated students who are eager to learn and contribute.

Who We’re Looking For

  • Undergraduate or graduate students with an interest in communication and cognition
  • Strong analytical skills and curiosity about human behavior
  • Commitment to collaborative research and open-mindedness

How to Apply

Please send the following materials to mark.lacour@louisiana.edu:

  • Your CV or resume
  • A brief statement of research interests and goals
  • Any relevant coursework or experience

What to Expect

New members typically start by assisting with ongoing projects, learning research methods, and contributing to lab meetings.

We provide mentorship and opportunities for independent projects as you grow.
