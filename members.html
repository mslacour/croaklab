Meet the Lab

Dr. Mark LaCour

Lab Director

I am Assistant Professor of Psychology at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. I earned a Ph.D. in experimental psychology from Texas Tech University with a concentration in cognition and cognitive neuroscience. Before that, I earned an M.S. in psychology and an honors B.A. in philosophy (minor in cognitive science) from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. I am primarily a cognitive/quantitative psychologist, though I also dabble in social psychology. My research focuses on judgment, decision-making, and belief processes with a special focus on public health issues (e.g., vaccine hesitancy, risk communication).
